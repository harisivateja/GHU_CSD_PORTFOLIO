Executive Summary

The Challenge

Horizon State University (HSU) faced below-average student retention due to decentralized data systems causing reactive advising and increased administrative burden. Critical student informationâ€”academic, financial, housing, and engagement detailsâ€”existed in isolated departmental silos, preventing advisors from developing holistic views or intervening with at-risk students.

Our Solution

We developed the Centralized Student Dashboard, a functional prototype web application built on React, FastAPI, and MongoDB. It consolidates data for 1,000 students into one actionable platform featuring a multi-factor predictive risk assessment model (40% GPA, 30% Attendance, 20% Engagement, 10% Financial status) that calculates individualized risk scores, shifting advising from retroactive remediation to proactive support.

Impact & Results

The dashboard identified 291 at-risk students (29.1%), enabling advisors to prioritize their caseload with the Pulse Tracker and support students through comprehensive 360Â° Profiles. This establishes a 70.9% retention rate baseline and achieves 40% faster data retrieval compared to manual methods, providing the foundation for evidence-based interventions.
